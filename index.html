Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any current enrollment discounts? ▼ Down arrow

We occasionally offer special enrollment discounts. Please check our pricing section or contact us directly for information on any current promotions.

When can my child start the program? ▼

We offer rolling enrollment throughout the year, subject to availability. Once your registration is complete, we will work with you to determine the best start date for your child.

What age groups do you accept? ▼

We accept children ages 3 months to 6 years old, with age-appropriate classrooms and activities for each developmental stage. Please see our 'Care Programs' section for more details.

Can I schedule a tour of the facilities? ▼

We encourage prospective families to visit our preschool. Please contact us via phone or email to schedule a personalized tour at your convenience.
